Fruits Custard

Image Goes Here

Iggridients Required

  • 2½ cups milk
  • custard powder
  • Fruits
  • Dry fruits
  • Sugar

Steps

  1. Pour 2½ cups milk to a heavy bottom pan.
  2. Add 3 to 4 tablespoons sugar to it.
  3. Begin to heat it on a medium flame. Stir and dissolve the sugar..
  4. when the milk turns hot (not boiling ).Pour some custurd powder to a one bowl and mix it properly with milk or water
  5. Add that mixed textured cream mix to the milk
  6. Now add the fruits to it(Apple, Bannana,Green grapes,Pomegranate )Just mix this well
  7. Now at last add some dry fruits to it for some flavour.
  8. Enjoy it..
